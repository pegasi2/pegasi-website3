What is Pegasi?

Pegasi is a Texas-based civil engineering consulting firm. We specialize in providing technical consulting, auxillary staffing support, subcontracting, and brand awareness for companies in the civil engineering, construction, and related fields. We also provide turnkey project management and civil engineering design services for clients in the municipal and private sectors. You can see our capabilities and project portfolio below.
